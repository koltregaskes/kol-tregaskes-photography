Kol Tregaskes

This site is now a curated archive of earlier photographic work. The pictures here were made across landscape, architecture, urban, and documentary photography.

The work tended toward the quiet and observational - finding composition in everyday structures, light in ordinary places, and the visual rhythms that most people walked past without noticing.

I worked mostly with natural light and favoured clean, considered compositions. The archive still leans into geometry in architecture, atmosphere in landscape, and honesty in documentary work.

I no longer work as an active photographer, and I am not taking commissions. This site now acts as the permanent home for the portfolio archive, with legacy links out to places like 500px and Redbubble where relevant.
