April 3, 2026

Welcome to Kol Tregaskes Photography

This site is being built as the long-term home for my photography work. The goal is simple: one place for the images themselves, one place for short written pieces about the process behind them, and one place for the wider photography world that I want to keep an eye on.

The gallery will hold the core portfolio work across landscape, architecture, urban scenes, documentary images, and quieter observational photographs. Over time that section will become more curated and more complete as the final image selection is added.

The blog is for notes from behind the lens: places, workflow, editing decisions, publishing thoughts, and the occasional piece about gear when it is genuinely useful. The idea is not to flood the site with filler, but to add context where it helps the photographs land better.

The news page is there to gather relevant photography updates in one place - camera releases, editing tools, industry shifts, and useful developments worth watching.

For now, this is the starting point. The framework is in place. The next step is filling it with the final image set and shaping it into the clean, focused portfolio it is meant to become.
